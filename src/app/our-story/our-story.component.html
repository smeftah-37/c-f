Building Legacies Since 1960: Excellence in Construction and Project Management

Our journey in the construction industry began in 1960, alongside some of the most revered figures in architecture and construction across the Kingdom of Morocco. From the pioneering leadership of Master Abdelkader ben Salem, Master Elie azagury, and Master Zevaco to the visionary contributions of Master Albert ben Chetrite, Mr ABdelali Bennis et fils, and others including Mohammed Fikri Ben abdellah, Hamid guessous, Rachid Andaloussi, Said lahlou, and Mostafa Driouech, we inherited a rich legacy of knowledge and expertise. This heritage, passed down through generations, forms the cornerstone of our identity. At C & F, we offer more than just consultancy and project management; we provide a transformative partnership throughout every critical phase of construction projects. Our commitment to excellence is grounded in the specialized discipline of Project Management (CPM), a pivotal skill that integrates cutting-edge tools and multidisciplinary knowledge. This approach ensures the successful execution of complex projects across diverse sectors including industrial, logistics, airport, medical, and hospitality.
